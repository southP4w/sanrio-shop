Our Story

Founded in 1960 by Shintaro Tsuji, Sanrio is a global lifestyle brand best known for its iconic character, Hello Kitty.

Our Mission

Sanrio's philosophy of "small gift, big smile" has made it a leading figure in the kawaii segment of Japanese popular culture.

Sanrio's characters, which include My Melody, Gudetama, and Keroppi, have captured the hearts of fans worldwide.
