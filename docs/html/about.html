About Sanrio

Our Story

Founded in 1960 by Shintaro Tsuji, Sanrio is a global lifestyle brand best known for its iconic character, Hello Kitty. Sanrio's philosophy of "small gift, big smile" has made it a leading figure in the kawaii segment of Japanese popular culture. Sanrio's characters, which include My Melody, Gudetama, and Keroppi, have captured the hearts of fans worldwide.

Our Mission

At Sanrio, we are dedicated to fostering social communication through the gift-giving and sharing of our adorable characters. Our mission is to inspire happiness and friendship across the globe, connecting people with the joy of kawaii culture.
