PomPomPurins Naphouse

Pompompurin is known for his laid-back and friendly nature. He loves milk and his mother's cream caramel puddings. Living in a basket at his sister-owner's house, he has friends like a hamster named Muffin and a mouse named Scone. He's depicted as loving naps and doing 'purin aerobics,' dreaming of getting bigger. His stories focus on friendship, kindness, and joy.
